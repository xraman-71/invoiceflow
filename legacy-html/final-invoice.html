Final Invoice
The definitive closing invoice issued upon project or order completion.
A Final Invoice is the conclusive billing document sent at the end of a project, contract, or job. It summarizes all work performed, deducts any previously issued advance or partial invoices, and states the remaining balance due. It formally closes the billing relationship for the engagement.
