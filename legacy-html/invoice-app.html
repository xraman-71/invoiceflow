The go-to document for any business transaction. Issue this for goods sold, services rendered, or freelance work completed. Accepted everywhere, by every industry.

TYPE 02 / 08 PRO
Proforma Invoice
A preliminary invoice before delivery or shipment.
Send before shipping goods or starting work. Used for customs declarations, import/export, advance payment collection, and confirming order details with buyers internationally.
Pre-Shipment Customs Import/Export Advance Payment

TYPE 03 / 08 COM
Commercial Invoice
Mandatory customs document for international trade.
The legally required document for every international shipment. Includes HS codes, port details, country of origin, Incoterms, and Bill of Lading — everything customs needs.
International Shipping Customs Clearance Import/Export Cross-Border
Create Commercial Invoice →
TYPE 04 / 08 TAX
Tax Invoice
VAT/GST compliant invoice for indirect tax systems.
Required in all GST/VAT countries — India, EU, UK, UAE, Australia, Canada and more. Includes tax registration numbers, HSN/SAC codes, and itemized tax for each line item for input credit claims.
GST (India/AU) VAT (EU/UK/UAE) Tax Credit Claims B2B Compliance
Create Tax Invoice →
TYPE 05 / 08 CRN
Credit Invoice (Credit Note)
Reduces the amount owed — the global refund document.
Issue when a buyer has overpaid, returned goods, or when you need to correct a billing error. The Credit Note references the original invoice and formally reduces the balance owed.
Returns & Refunds Overbilling Fix Post-Sale Discounts Cancellations
Create Credit Note →
TYPE 06 / 08 DBN
Debit Invoice (Debit Note)
Increases the amount owed — a global adjustment document.
Issue when a buyer owes more than originally invoiced — for extra work, price adjustments, penalties, or underbilling corrections. References the original invoice and formally increases the balance due.
Underbilling Fix Additional Scope Price Adjustments Late Fee / Penalty
Create Debit Note →
TYPE 07 / 08 REC
Recurring Invoice
Periodic invoicing for subscriptions and ongoing services.
For monthly retainers, SaaS subscriptions, utility billing, or any regular service. Set the billing frequency, period from/to, and contract ID. Tracks next invoice date automatically.
SaaS Subscriptions Monthly Retainers Utility Billing Service Contracts
Create Recurring Invoice →
TYPE 08 / 08 FIN
Final Invoice
The closing invoice issued on project or contract completion.
The conclusive billing document at the end of a project or contract. Deducts all previously invoiced amounts and advances paid, showing only the remaining balance due. Formally closes the billing engagement.
Project Closeout Construction Consulting End Final Milestone
Create Final Invoice →
